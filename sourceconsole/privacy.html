Privacy First

SourceConsole Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. SourceConsole is designed from the ground up to keep your data private and secure. No accounts, no servers, no tracking - just a simple tool that works entirely on your device.

Last updated: January 2025

What We Don't Collect

SourceConsole operates on a simple principle: we don't want your data, so we don't collect it. Here's what stays private:

  • Browsing History. The websites you visit, pages you test, and URLs you enter remain completely private on your device.
  • Personal Information. No accounts, no sign-ups, no personal data collection of any kind.
  • Usage Analytics. We don't track how you use the app, what features you access, or how often you use it.

How SourceConsole Works

SourceConsole operates entirely on your device. When you load a webpage, the connection goes directly from your device to that website - we're not in the middle. Think of it like using Safari or Chrome, but specifically designed for testing and development.

Mac Connection (Optional)

When you choose to connect SourceConsole to your Mac for deeper inspection, the connection is direct between your iOS device and your Mac over your local network. No data passes through our servers or any third-party services.

  • Local Network Only. Mac connections use your local WiFi network - no internet servers involved.
  • You Control Everything. You decide what to share, when to connect, and can disconnect anytime.

Third-Party Websites

When you visit websites using SourceConsole, those websites may have their own privacy policies and data collection practices. SourceConsole doesn't modify or interfere with how those websites work - we just display them for you to test and inspect.

App Store & System Data

Apple may collect standard App Store analytics (like download counts) as part of their platform. SourceConsole doesn't add any additional tracking beyond what iOS provides by default. You can control iOS-level analytics in your device's Privacy & Security settings.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever need to update this privacy policy, we'll do so through an app update with clear information about what changed. Our core commitment to privacy won't change - SourceConsole will always be designed to keep your data on your device.

Questions?

If you have questions about privacy or how SourceConsole works, you can reach out through the App Store or our support channels. We're happy to explain our approach and help you understand exactly what happens with your data (spoiler: nothing!).

The Bottom Line

SourceConsole is a tool, not a service. Like a hammer or a calculator, it does its job without needing to know anything about you. Your testing stays private, your data stays local, and your workflow stays uninterrupted.
